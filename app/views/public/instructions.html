Instructions

To start visit www.openwatertracker.com in your mobile phone or tablet browser. We support Apple, Android and Windows devices.

Login



Use the following to login to Open Water Tracker:
username: user@example.com (not your own email!)
password: openwater


Check In



  • Search for swimmer by last name.
  • Click the CHECK IN button to check in the swimmer. If button is labled "Setup" please see below.
  • Click the SETUP button, this will open a pop up where you can enter in the swimmers 10-digit phone number (with no dashes). The swimmer will need to review the waiver provided on-site and acknowledge they agree to it by clicking the "Agree" button". Once the swimmer has agreed to the waiver click CHECK IN to check the swimmer in.

Check Out



  • Search for swimmer by last name or scroll down to find the correct swimmer.
  • Click the CHECK OUT button to check out the swimmer.
  • If you need to call the swimmer simply click CALL.
